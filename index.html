About Me

I approach analytics as both a technical and business problem: clean data, strong logic, and insights that people can actually use.

Who I Am

I’m a data analyst with an engineering background, a master’s degree in Information Technology and Analytics from RIT, and professional experience at TCS. I enjoy working with data to understand what is actually happening behind the numbers. My work focuses on breaking down complex problems, organizing messy data, and turning analysis into insights that help teams make better decisions.

My Approach

I like working across the full analytics process. That means starting with the business question, checking and validating the data, defining meaningful KPIs, and then building dashboards or analysis that people can easily understand. I care a lot about clean data structures, reliable analysis, and making sure the final output is practical and useful for stakeholders.
